Welcome! I'm Your Host, Jack Canfield

You may know me as the co-founder of the Chicken Soup for the Soul brand or author of several New York Times bestselling books on self-improvement, such as The Aladdin Factor, The Power of Focus, and The Success Principles: How to Get from Where You Are to Where You Want to Be.

From my earliest days teaching inner-city high school students how to discover their potential and succeed at life — to becoming a world-renowned transformational speaker and trainer — I have devoted my life and career to helping others achieve their personal definition of success and create lives of greater joy, and fulfilment.

Now I want to help you unlock your own capacity for success and help you discover how incredible your life can be!
