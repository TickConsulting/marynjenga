Meet Mary Njenga

Award-Winning Speaker & Internationally Recognized Leader in Personal Development

Empowering People to Live their Highest Vision

I'm Mary Njenga,a corporate and life coach with over 10 years of experience helping leaders,entrepreneurs,graduate trainees, and high-achieving professionals reach their full potential. My approach blends proven coaching techniques with a deep understanding of human behaviour, organizational dynamics, and personal development.

I've worked with clients across industries- from startups to established corporations-empowering them to overcome challenges, build resilience, and develop strategies for success. However, beyond professional achievement, I believe in nurturing a holistic approach that integrates well-being, fulfilment, and personal growth.

I will impose no identities upon you but will invite you to become yourself without shame or fear. I reiterate that I will hold a safe space for you in the world and allow your right to fill it with an authentic vocation and purpose.

In the words of Theodore Roszak: "For as long as your search takes, you have my loyalty."
